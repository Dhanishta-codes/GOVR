Control Architecture

Five signals.
One decision.

No single signal controls execution. All five must converge before the system proceeds.

Risk
Risk Detection
Detects guardrail failures, retry instability, missing outputs, and execution gaps. Every anomaly is scored before it propagates.
Dependency
Structural Integrity
Hard checks: research missing, email without persona, contacts without email. Soft scoring for qualification weakness and fallback usage.
Confidence
Reliability Signal
One number — derived from workflow score, business score, benchmark signal, and instability indicators. How much to trust this run.
Threshold
Adaptive Control
Thresholds evolve with performance history. Strong track record earns wider autonomy. Underperformance tightens the gate.
Decision
Single Authority
The final arbiter. Fuses all four signals into one governance decision. Nothing proceeds without it. The enterprise safety gate.
Adaptive Thresholds

The system learns
its own posture.

Conservative
Governance Tightens
When historical performance is weak, thresholds become stricter. The system demands more confidence before granting any degree of autonomy.
Triggered by: underperformance history, high retry rate, consecutive guardrail failures
Neutral
Balanced Control
Default posture. Standard thresholds apply. The system is observing — neither expanding nor contracting autonomy. Waiting for signal.
Current state: average performance maintained, no significant deviations detected
Aggressive
Autonomy Expands
Sustained strong performance earns wider thresholds. The system earns the right to act with less friction — through track record, not configuration.
Triggered by: strong performance history, stable confidence, zero guardrail failures
Human Oversight
Autonomy is mediated.
Never absolute.
Regardless of confidence score or governance signal — a human can always intervene. Every override is logged. Every decision leaves a trail. Control is never sacrificed for speed.
01
Manual Approval
Flag any execution for human review before it proceeds. Works at any stage in the pipeline.
02
Override Logging
Every human intervention logged with timestamp, actor, reason, and governance state at time of override.
03
Decision Audit Trail
Full history of every decision — automated and manual — stored and queryable. Enterprise compliance ready.
